Insalata Caprese
  • Shopping List
  • Cookbook
  • Preferences

Ingredients

4 tomatoes, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1 lb fresh mozzarella, sliced 1/4 inch thick
1/3 cup fresh basil leaves
3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
to taste sea salt
to taste black pepper

Directions/Notes

On a large platter, alternate and overlap the tomato slices, mozzarella cheese slices, and basil leaves. Drizzle with olive oil. Season with sea salt and pepper.
